Biography / History

Marcello Nizzoli was an industrial designer, architect and painter, among the most important of the Italian twentieth century, the father of the most famous Olivetti typewriters, symbol of Italian modernity and industrial rebirth non-war, with objects also exhibited at the MOMA in New York.
