LEXIKON 80

The Italian art critic and philosopher, Gillo Dorfles, defined the Lexikon 80 typewriter in the most striking way,


“[The Lexikon 80] is one of the first examples of a bodywork object whose sleek lines were conceived not only for aesthetic purposes, but also with the intention to create a correspondence with the internal structure mechanism itself”. (1957)
